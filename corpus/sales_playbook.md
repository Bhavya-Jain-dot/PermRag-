# Sales Discovery Playbook

Discovery calls should identify the customer's business objective, timeline, decision process, and existing workflow. Use the approved case studies and product capability sheets rather than making unverified product claims.

Record qualified opportunities in the CRM within one business day. Bring a solutions engineer into technical validation when the buyer needs architecture, integration, or security detail.

Renewal conversations should begin at least 120 days before the contract end date so the account team has time to align on outcomes and adoption plans.
