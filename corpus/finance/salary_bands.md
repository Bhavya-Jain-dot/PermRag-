# FY2026 Salary Bands — Confidential

The approved FY2026 base-salary bands are confidential. Engineering level E2 has a range of $95,000 to $118,000. Engineering level E3 has a range of $122,000 to $148,000. Engineering level E4 has a range of $154,000 to $186,000.

Sales account executive base salary has a range of $78,000 to $105,000, with variable compensation governed by the signed incentive plan. Only Finance, HR compensation partners, and administrators may use this document when planning offers or adjustments.

Do not share compensation ranges in chat, screenshots, or external documents. Direct questions to the compensation review queue.
