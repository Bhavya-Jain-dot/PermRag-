# Q3 Operating Budget — Confidential

The approved Q3 operating budget is $4.2 million. Product infrastructure receives $1.35 million, go-to-market programs receive $1.10 million, and the remaining budget covers operations, customer support, and contingency reserves.

Department owners should submit variance explanations when a monthly spend forecast changes by more than five percent. Finance publishes the consolidated forecast on the third business day of each month.

Budget worksheets and vendor-level commitments are confidential finance records and may not be shared outside the Finance team without written approval.
