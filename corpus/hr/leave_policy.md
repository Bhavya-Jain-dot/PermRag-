# Leave and Time-Off Policy

All regular employees receive 24 paid days of annual leave per calendar year. Submit planned leave in the team calendar at least two weeks in advance when practical, then ask your manager to approve it in the HR portal.

Employees may take up to 10 paid sick days annually. For absences longer than three consecutive workdays, contact People Operations so they can explain the documentation and return-to-work process.

The company observes the published regional holiday calendar. Unused annual leave may carry forward for one quarter, subject to local employment law and manager approval.
