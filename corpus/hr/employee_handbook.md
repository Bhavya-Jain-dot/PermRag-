# Employee Handbook

The employee handbook explains workplace conduct, onboarding, feedback cycles, and reporting channels. New team members receive an orientation during their first week and may ask People Operations for accessibility accommodations.

Managers should discuss performance feedback privately, document agreed actions, and contact HR when an employee raises a concern about discrimination, harassment, or unsafe working conditions. Personal employee records are confidential and are available only to HR and authorized administrators.

The company supports flexible work arrangements where the job can be performed safely and effectively. Teams agree their collaboration hours at the beginning of each quarter.
